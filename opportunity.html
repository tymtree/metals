Affiliate: Someone who enrolls with the annual $29 administration fee.

Definitions, Terms and Policies:

Academy Member (AM)

Every Academy Member has the option of starting their 22K Metals business with either one Academy membership ($300 + $29 admin fee) or three Academy memberships ($900 + $87 admin fee). The 3 Academy memberships option will give them leverage to earn additional income with less work. The number two and the number three Academy memberships cannot be open or earn until the number one has earned the maximum payout of $1000 in a day at least once.


Each Academy Member (AM) will receive 200 in personal volume (PV) per Academy membership that will be attached to their new business.


Each Academy Member (AM) will also receive a free $300 rebate credit per Academy membership that will be applied to earning their personal set of twelve one ounce .999% silver coins.


Example - A set of 12 one ounce .999% silver coins is sold for $800. An Academy Member receives a $300 rebate credit towards the 12 set.

The first $500 that an AM earns in the Dual Team Commission Bonus and will be applied to the unpaid balance and only then their 12 one ounce .999% silver coins will be shipped to them the following Friday.


Academy Introduction Bonus (AIB):

An active 22K Metals AM that makes an AM sale of $300 will receive a 15% academy introduction bonus of $45.

* The 15% Academy Introduction Bonus is always paid out in Bitcoin the next Friday.

Activate: An AM activates their business by selling 1 AM on their left and right teams. Once this is complete your 22K Metals business is now Active and eligible to earn commissions.

To be eligible to earn additional commissions a 22K Metals AM must: 1. Maintain a minimum of one active personally enrolled AM on the left and right team. 2. Must generate a minimum of one $40 sale or purchase from their personal website or back office every thirty days. This $40 sale or purchase will represent 20 in BV and it will be placed and counted in their lesser pay team. Example - If an AM becomes active on May 25th then he/she would need to make one $40 sale or purchase before June 24th to remain active. *Prices are plus shipping and handling to be paid for by the customer receiving the product. Dual Team Commissions: A qualified and active AM will earn 15% of their lesser teams volume for each pay period. *The start of each pay period is Monday morning at 12:00am central standard time and ends on Sunday at 11:59 pm central standard time. *Commissions will be paid out five days after each pay period has ended on the following Friday afternoon. *Team commissions are calculated daily and are capped at $1000 per day. "IF A 22K METALS AM BECOMES INACTIVE BY FAILING TO MAKE AT LEAST ONE SALE OR PURCHASE OF $40 IN ANY CALENDAR MONTH THEY WILL NOT BE ELIGIBLE TO EARN COMMISSIONS UNTIL COMPLETING THAT SALE OR PURCHASE AND ALL OF THEIR ESCROWED VOLUME WILL BE FLUSHED."
